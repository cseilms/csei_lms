Progression

The Progression+ team of CSEI Academy includes experts in admissions, careers, higher education, work experience and apprenticeships who offer advice and guidance. They can help with all aspects of careers and progression which includes applying for courses, interview preparation classes, resume writing, application forms, job and apprenticeship searches, and gap year opportunities.
