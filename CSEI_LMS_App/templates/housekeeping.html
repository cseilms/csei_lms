Training Diploma in Housekeeping

This is a short Course on Housekeeping designed and developed by housekeeping industry specialists who have been in the business for years. The Course covers the various aspects of housekeeping to gain valuable insights into the world of Housekeeping.

Total Course Duration: 96 hours

Learning Objectives

At the end of the Course, the learner will:

  • Be able to perform housekeeping operations efficiently in the hospitality industry.
  • Learn in detail the Hotel Guest Room preparation.
  • Be able to use different cleaning equipment and understand cleaning science.

Course Outline

  • Basics of Housekeeping Operations
  • Housekeeping operation in Hotel Guest Room
  • Essential use of Cleaning Science & Cleaning Equipment
  • Care & Cleaning of Different Surfaces

Course Benefit

This program will award you a Training Diploma in Housekeeping Completion Certificate from the Academy. Upon successful completion of 96 hours of course duration, learners will also receive a certification from KHDA. After completion, students can also join OTHM courses related to their field, such as Tourism & Hospitality Management, as part of progression to the course.

Required Documents

  • Higher Academic Certificates
  • Passport copy
  • Photograph
  • Local identity (Citizenship)
  • Experience letter (Optional)

Requirements

  • Students must demonstrate evidence of numeracy and literacy skills through last studied high school report.
  • Work experience demonstrated through an updated resume (for working professionals).
  • Additional English language proficiency may apply where the medium of teaching is not English.

Study Mode

  • Full-Time / Part-Time: Weekend based
  • Location: ON CAMPUS

Career Opportunities

The certificate holder can start their careers as Housekeeping Associates or Room Attendants in the hotel & hospitality industry or in cruise liners & tourism industry. With experience, they can then move up the ranks to positions like Floor Supervisor, Executive Housekeeper, and eventually, Director of Housekeeping.
