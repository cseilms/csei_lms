Travelling to CSEI Academy

Car & Motor Cycle Parking

The CSEI Academy Dubai campus is located in the Dubai International Academic City (DIAC) zone. Our campus is near to Dubai International Airport and 30 minutes from EXPO City. Every effort has been made to ensure that the information supplied is correct at the time of publication. Students and their parents should check with service providers to ensure that published costs and timetables are current and correct for transportation.

Walking & Cycling

There are pedestrian and bicycle accesses to the institute at Academic City Block 12, Campus 303 as well as our other Admission Centre located at Speedex Centre, near Abu Baker Metro Station, Deira, Dubai. The Academic City is located at Dubai Academic City Zone that has entrances leading to a well-lit pedestrian footpath to the main building and car parking area of Dubai International Academic City. There are bicycle spaces on site close to the main entrance.

Who Manages the College Car Parks?

Dubai International Academic City is accessible by metro, public bus, taxi, and ride-hailing services. There are various convenient metered parking options in and around Dubai International Academic City.

Parking

There are various convenient metered parking options in and around Dubai International Academic City. Car parking services are part of the Campus Services team who are responsible for the management of campus car parking.

Staff
