Food Safety and Sanitation

This is a basic Food safety & Sanitization that complies with Dubai Municipality regulation. This course is mandatory for anyone working in food establishments and ensures that personnel are adequately trained in food safety protocols.

Course Duration: 35 hours

Learning Objectives

At the end of the Food Safety & Sanitization course:

  • Awareness of food safety
  • Ability to perform proper sanitization, storage, preparation, and serving of food.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to Food Safety
  • Benefits of Good Food Hygiene
  • Costs of Poor Food Hygiene
  • Food Safety Hazards and their Control
  • Factors for Bacterial Growth and their Control
  • Food Safety Control Measures in Operation
  • Personal Hygiene Practices
  • Pest Control Methods and Prevention of Pest Infestation
  • Waste Disposal
  • Cleaning and Disinfection
  • Food Safety

Course Benefit

This program will award you a Food Safety and Sanitation Completion Certificate from the Academy. Upon successful completion of 35 hours of course duration, learners will also receive certification from KHDA.

Required Documents

  • Higher Academic Certificates
  • Passport copy
  • Photograph
  • Local identity (Citizenship)
  • Experience letter (Optional)

Requirements

  • Students must demonstrate evidence of numeracy and literacy skills through their last studied high school report.
  • Work experience demonstrated through an updated resume (for working professionals).
  • Additional English language proficiency may apply where the medium of teaching is not English.

Study Mode

  • Full-Time / Part-Time: Weekend based
  • Location: ON CAMPUS

Career Opportunities

This course is essential for learners seeking certification in food safety and sanitization; it's mandatory for restaurant staff and individuals handling food in kitchen departments of hotels or restaurants.
