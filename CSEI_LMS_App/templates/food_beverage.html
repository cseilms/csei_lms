Food & Beverage Service Training

Food & Beverage Service Training and Sales Techniques provide the fundamentals needed for a solid foundation in Food & Beverage Service. Upon completion of this course, students will have the skills needed to work autonomously and/or in a team environment in the service industry.

Course Duration: 96 hours

Learning Objectives

At the end of the course, learners will be able to:

  • Demonstrate appropriate service behaviors for various guest types.
  • Understand the concept and techniques of good service and demonstrate the skills acquired.
  • Program point-of-sale technology for service.
  • Participate in the operation and execution of in-room dining service.
  • Participate in the operation and execution of banquet dining service.
  • Participate in the operation and execution of restaurant dining services.
  • Provide responsible service of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.
  • Demonstrate the English service technique.
  • Demonstrate American Service technique.
  • Demonstrate the Russian Service technique.
  • Demonstrate Continental Service technique.

Course Outline

  • Standard Operating Procedures for Breakfast, Buffet, and Brunch Service
  • POS System Functioning
  • Effective Use of Trolley Service
  • Characteristics of Basic and Informal F&B
  • Work in accordance with the legislation regulating F & B
  • Efficient Order Taking

Course Benefit

This program will award you a Food & Beverage Service Training Completion Certificate from the Academy. Upon successful completion of 96 hours of course duration, learners will also receive certification from KHDA. The student after completion can also join OTHM courses related to their field in Tourism & Hospitality Management as a part of Progression to the course.

Requirements

  • Work experience shown through an updated resume (for professionals).
  • Additional English language proficiency may be required if the medium of teaching is not English.

Career Opportunities

The learner can excel & get certified & pursue a career as a Senior Steward, Food Service or Restaurant Manager, Beverage Services Manager, Dining Room Manager, Food Production Manager & other various fields related to the F & B department of the hotel & hospitality industry.
