Your Privacy Matters

At CSEI Academy, we understand the importance of your privacy, and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we collect, use, and protect your data in a manner that respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with the best academic coaching experience:

  • Personal Details: This may include your name, email address, phone number, and other contact information, which we use to stay in touch with you.
  • Academic Insights: To offer tailored coaching services, we may collect academic information about you or your child, such as grade levels and subjects of interest.
  • Secure Payments: When you make payments for our services, we collect only the necessary payment details to process transactions securely.
  • Website Interaction: We gather data about your interactions with our website, including your device type and pages you visit. This helps us enhance your browsing experience.
How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • Personalized Coaching: To deliver coaching that meets your unique needs.
  • Payment Processing: To handle payments and manage billing efficiently.
  • Website Improvement: To enhance the functionality and usability of our website.
  • Communication: To keep you informed about appointments, inquiries, and updates.
  • Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements.
Sharing Your Information

We may share your information under specific circumstances:

  • Service Partners: We work with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our services effectively.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of others.
  • Business Transitions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to another entity.
Your Choices

We respect your choices regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Corrections: You can access and correct your data as needed.
  • Consent Withdrawal: You have the right to withdraw your consent for data collection and processing.
  • Marketing Opt-Out: You can choose to opt out of receiving marketing communications from us.
Data Security

While we employ security measures to protect your information, please note that no internet transmission can be entirely secure.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. Please review it occasionally to stay informed about how we handle your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy or this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at admission@cseiacademy.ae.
