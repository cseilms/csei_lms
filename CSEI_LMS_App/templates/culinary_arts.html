Culinary Arts Program

The Culinary Arts Program is designed for aspiring chefs and culinary enthusiasts who wish to develop foundational skills and advanced techniques in the culinary field.

Course Overview

This comprehensive program combines hands-on experience, theoretical knowledge, and exposure to the culinary industry. Participants will learn about ingredients, cooking methods, food safety, plating techniques, and menu design, preparing them for various roles within the culinary sector.

Course Duration: 1 Month (Part-Time)

Tuition Fees: 17,000 AED

Course Objectives

  • Demonstrate fundamental cooking techniques and skills.
  • Understand the properties and functions of various ingredients.
  • Apply food safety and sanitation practices.
  • Create and design menus for different cuisines and dining experiences.
  • Develop skills in pastry, baking, and dessert-making.
  • Understand market trends and customer preferences in the culinary industry.

Modules

  • Introduction to Culinary Arts
  • Kitchen Fundamentals
  • Food Safety and Sanitation
  • Culinary Techniques
  • Baking and Pastry Arts
  • Nutrition and Menu Planning
  • Global Cuisines
  • Plating and Presentation
  • Culinary Management
  • Capstone Project

Assessment and Certification

Continuous assessment through practical exams, written tests, and participation. Certification awarded upon successful completion of the program.

Career Opportunities

  • Chef or Cook
  • Pastry Chef
  • Food Stylist or Photographer
  • Restaurant Management
  • Catering and Event Planning
  • Food and Beverage Consultant
