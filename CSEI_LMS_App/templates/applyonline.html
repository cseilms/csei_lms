Application Process

CSEI will personalize your journey according to your needs. We start this process as soon as you contact us and our support continues even after the completion of your course. Each step of the application process will be guided by a dedicated enrollment advisor or consultant for you and your parents. They can assist you with finances, scholarships, campus locations, and application procedures. Your visa coordinator will also work closely with you to ensure a safe arrival on campus. You can rely on our team of international education specialists to ensure a stress-free and smooth transition to college.

What We Look For

Our mission at CSEI is to get to know you and what drives you towards your goal. Our goal is to hire students who will make a difference not just in the business world, but in the entire world; students who exhibit passion, curiosity, leadership potential, and drive are welcome to CSEI Academy . Every application undergoes a comprehensive review based on the information provided. We also believe in education for all, so we help each student overcome to their weaknesses and make them ready for the future.

Our Curriculum

Through real-world business scenarios, simulations, theories, and the right industry partners, what you learn will be put into practice. As you prepare to become a successful global professional, you will work in diverse teams, take risks in a safe and supportive environment, and solve challenges businesses are facing right now. With the help of the latest knowledge and technologies, we have identified the professional and technical skills global employers seek when hiring and have incorporated them directly into our programs. In order to graduate with the skillset and knowledge you need for the role you desire, we’ll work together, continuously, to build an essential knowledge base, mindsets, and skills.
