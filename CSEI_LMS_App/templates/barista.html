Barista Training Course

Basic Barista Course

The Basic Barista Course is a beginner-level training program designed for individuals who are interested in pursuing a career as a barista or simply want to learn the art of making great coffee.

Course Duration: 20 hours

Learning Objectives

  • You will have a strong foundation in coffee preparation, including the skills and knowledge necessary to make high-quality espresso drinks and provide excellent customer service.
  • You will gain hands-on experience through both classroom instruction and practical training on espresso machines and milk steaming equipment.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to coffee
  • Milk Preparation
  • Espresso preparation
  • Drink preparation
  • Customer service

Course Benefit

This program will award you a Basic Barista Course Completion Certificate from the Institute.

Career Opportunities

The learner might choose career prospects as a professional barista, with opportunities to work in a restaurant, café, coffee shop, or hotel. Many large cities have gourmet coffee carts that require skilled baristas.

Advanced Barista Training Course

The Advanced Barista Course is designed for baristas who have completed a Basic Barista Course and want to advance their skills in coffee preparation and management.

Course Duration: 20 hours

Learning Objectives

  • You will learn advanced skills in coffee preparation and the management and leadership skills necessary to run a successful coffee shop.
  • You will have hands-on training that includes classroom instruction and practical training on espresso machines, milk steaming equipment, and different coffee brewing methods.

Course Outline

  • Espresso theory and practice
  • Milk science and latte art
  • Coffee brewing methods
  • Menu development and profitability
  • Management and leadership

Course Benefit

This program will award you an Advanced Barista Course Completion Certificate from the Academy. Learners will also receive a certification from KHDA upon completion.

Career Opportunities

Completing the Advanced Barista Course can prepare you to operate your café and provide you with the knowledge and experience of a professional barista.

Required Documents

  • Higher Academic Certificates
  • Passport copy
  • Photograph
  • Local identity (Citizenship)
  • Experience letter (Optional)

Requirements

  • Students must demonstrate evidence of numeracy and literacy skills through their last studied high school report.
  • Additional English language proficiency may apply if the medium of teaching is not English.

Study Mode

  • Full-Time / Part-Time: Weekend based
  • Location: ON CAMPUS
